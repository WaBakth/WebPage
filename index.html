Introduction

Louis Vuitton is a French luxury fashion house founded in 1854, known globally for its iconic LV monogram and exclusive designs in bags, clothing, and accessories.

History

Founded by Louis Vuitton himself, the brand began with high-quality trunks and luggage, and has since evolved into one of the most powerful luxury brands in the world.

Products

Louis Vuitton offers a wide range of luxury products including handbags, shoes, watches, jewelry, ready-to-wear fashion, and fragrances.

Brand Ambassadors

Louis Vuitton has worked with high-profile ambassadors such as BTS, Alicia Vikander, Léa Seydoux, and Stray Kids' Felix, bringing their fashion to global youth and luxury culture.

Global Influence

With boutiques in over 60 countries and a massive influence on high fashion, LV has become more than a brand—it's a lifestyle statement.
